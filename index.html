About Me

I have been a passionate developer since I was 14. My focus mainly being on game development, however as time went on my desire to learn become an insatiable hunger. I wanted to expand my knowledge base. So while working at SkillsVR, I used opportunities there to learn more about what I wanted from my future.

It was there that I further embraced my hunger to learn. The more I think about it, the more I realize I was never just a Game Developer, or even a just a developer at all.
